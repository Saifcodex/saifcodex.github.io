I have cultivated a strong foundation in computer science, constantly challenging myself through rigorous courses and projects. My dedication to learning and problem-solving has been evident in my pursuit of competitive programming. I am driven by a passion for technology and aspire to contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of the tech industry. My goal is to join a tech giant company where I can leverage my skills to shape the future of technology.
